GopherCon 2014

The Go programming language is taking off. The people who were waiting for early adopters to prove out the language are flocking in droves to see why great companies like Google, Bit.ly, Heroku, Iron.io, SoundCloud, and Canonical are putting their weight behind Go. The time is right to start building a well-organized community behind the programming language.

GopherCon is the inaugural event for an annual conference devoted exclusively to the Go programming language.

Gopher Academy

Gopher Academy's mission is to foster a community of Go developers by providing education, training, and events.
